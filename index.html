About RAPID

RAPID is a research group based at the Centre for Quality Management of Medicines, Faculty of Pharmacy, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).

We apply pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, and clinical pharmacology to optimise drug dosing and improve patient outcomes, with a focus on thrombosis & haemostasis, oncology, and antibiotics.

Research Focus

  • Thrombosis & Haemostasis – anticoagulants and precision dosing
  • Oncology – dosing optimisation and TDM
  • Antibiotics – population PK and model-informed dosing
