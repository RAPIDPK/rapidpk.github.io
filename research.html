Thrombosis & Haemostasis

Research focuses on pharmacokinetic and pharmacometric approaches to optimise anticoagulant therapy and improve outcomes in patients with thrombotic disorders.

Oncology

We investigate dosing strategies and therapeutic drug monitoring for anticancer and supportive care medications to enhance safety and effectiveness.

Antibiotics

Our work includes population pharmacokinetics and model-informed dosing of antimicrobials in special populations such as critically ill patients.
