Journal Club

RAPID runs regular journal club sessions focused on pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, clinical pharmacology, and precision dosing.

Typical topics:

  • Population PK and model-informed precision dosing
  • Therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM)
  • Special populations and critical care
  • Pharmacogenetics and emerging methods
