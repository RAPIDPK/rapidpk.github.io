Who we are

RAPID is a collaborative research group based at the Centre for Quality Management of Medicines, Faculty of Pharmacy, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).

We work with clinicians and researchers to improve dosing decisions using applied pharmacokinetics, pharmacometrics, pharmacogenetics, and assay-linked translational research.

Our focus is on translating quantitative pharmacology into real-world clinical impact, particularly in complex patient populations in Malaysia and the region.
